How performance and reward practices shape long-term business results

This project links employee lifecycle, effective-dated compensation and performance history, and business-unit KPIs to produce decision-ready outputs: attrition risk, next-period revenue forecasts, and KPI what-if impact estimates.

What this answers

Leaders make performance decisions constantly: promotions, merit, recognition, development, and improvement actions. This model asks whether those practices are producing the workforce the business needs today, and what that means in dollars.

Decision support focus

  • Where is revenue exposed due to workforce instability?
  • Which operational levers move next-period revenue most?
  • How do changes in staffing dynamics translate into future outcomes?

Core outputs

Attrition risk
Employee-month risk scores using a stay probability model
Revenue forecast
Next-period BU revenue prediction from aggregated workforce features
KPI what-if impacts
Dollar deltas from controlled KPI changes using the trained revenue model

Method summary

  • Time-aware snapshots: employee-month and BU-month panels that avoid future leakage.
  • Effective-dated joins: groupwise as-of attachment of compensation and performance history.
  • Stay modeling: learn P(stay) over a forward horizon; derive attrition risk as 1 − P(stay).
  • BU-level revenue model: prevent duplication bias by forecasting revenue at BU-period level.
  • What-if analysis: reuse revenue model to price KPI changes into next-period dollars.

Interpretation guardrails

  • Outputs are decision support, not causal proof.
  • What-if impacts are model-based marginal estimates.
  • Short-term effects can be negative for investments with delayed payoff.
  • Use concentration and lift for action targeting, not accuracy alone.

Outputs and what they mean

  • panel_scored.csv: employee-month panel with features and risk scores.
  • panel_scores_min.csv: minimal employee-month scoring output for action lists.
  • bu_period_features.csv: aggregated BU-period feature set used for revenue forecasting.
  • bu_period_scored.csv: BU-period revenue predictions and residuals.
  • kpi_impact_output.csv: BU-period predicted revenue deltas per KPI change.
  • kpi_impact_summary.csv: summary statistics (mean, p10, p50, p90) per KPI impact.

Dashboard pages

  • Executive overview: predicted revenue, revenue-at-risk, expected exits, risk concentration.
  • Revenue at risk: risk distribution, risk tiers, BU concentration, intervention list.
  • KPI impact: ranked levers, what-if deltas in dollars, sensitivity summary.
  • Workforce shaping: promotions, merit, performance patterns over time vs outcomes.
  • Scenarios: combine levers to project revenue impact and tradeoffs.

Revenue-at-risk calculation

This converts attrition risk into expected revenue exposure at the BU-period level.

import pandas as pd

panel = pd.read_csv("panel_scored.csv")
bu = pd.read_csv("bu_period_scored.csv")

df = panel.merge(
  bu[["business_unit","period","predicted_revenue_next_period","headcount"]],
  on=["business_unit","period"], how="left"
)

df["revenue_exposure"] = df["predicted_revenue_next_period"] / df["headcount"]
df["revenue_risk"] = df["attrition_risk"] * df["revenue_exposure"]

print("Total Revenue at Risk:", df["revenue_risk"].sum())
