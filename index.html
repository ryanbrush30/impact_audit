Impact Audit

A multi-year workforce analytics framework that connects performance, compensation, and attrition decisions to business KPIs and future revenue.

The Problem

Organizations promote, reward, and manage performance every year — but rarely measure whether those decisions actually produce the workforce and results they need long-term.

The Solution

Impact Audit reconstructs workforce history over time and models how people practices shape KPIs and revenue — not just immediately, but months and years later.

What It Models

Attrition Risk Revenue Forecasting KPI Impact Workforce Strategy Decision Governance

Why It Matters

This is not HR analytics. This is workforce capital allocation analysis.
