Purpose and intent

ImpactAudit v2 is a modular analytics framework designed to quantify how workforce risk translates into measurable business and operational exposure.

It prioritizes interpretability, uncertainty awareness, and conservative conclusions. The system is designed for executive decision support and finance partnership, not automation.

Important: This system is correlation-aware, not causal. Outputs are decision support signals, not guarantees.

What this system does and does not do

It does

  • Produce workforce risk signals suitable for planning and prioritization
  • Aggregate employee-level signals into business-unit and period KPIs
  • Translate people risk into finance-relevant exposure language
  • Support scenario analysis and sensitivity testing
  • Generate reproducible artifacts with manifests and hashes for audit

It does not

  • Make causal claims
  • Provide individual prescriptions or automated employment actions
  • Serve as a performance management or disciplinary tool
  • Guarantee outcomes
Prohibited use: Do not use ImpactAudit outputs to support hiring, firing, promotion, demotion, discipline, termination, or compensation decisions for individuals.

Current architecture (v2)

  • Stage 1: Employee-level attrition risk modeling (time-aware validation)
  • Stage 2: Business-unit and period aggregation (interpretability layer)
  • Stage 3: Revenue and KPI exposure modeling (production)

Each stage produces versioned, immutable artifacts with explicit configuration manifests. Rollback is supported and treated as a normal control.

Versioning and evolution

  • v1: Earlier, more coupled scripts and limited auditability
  • v2 (current): Modular pipeline, strict time splits, versioned artifacts, leakage audits, governance posture
  • v3 (experimental): Explored, then rolled back intentionally after validation signals did not meet standards
Note: Rollback is a control. It reflects prioritizing validity and safety over inflated metrics.
